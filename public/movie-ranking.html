The Best Movies According to Iqbal

My top 3 movies of all-time.

The Hangover

Directed by Todd Phillips, this comedy about a bachelor party gone wrong is famous for its hilarious situations and ensemble cast performances.

The Dark Knight

Directed by Christopher Nolan, this film is highly acclaimed for its intense action, gripping storyline, and Heath Ledger's iconic performance as the Joker.

Interstellar

Directed by Christopher Nolan, this film is celebrated for its stunning visuals and complex storytelling about space exploration and the survival of humanity.
